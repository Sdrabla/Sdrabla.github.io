Project Ranked

I built a web app that allows users to rank their favorite anime, manga, and TV shows. The idea came from my own frustration with existing platforms, while many offer similar functionality, I found them difficult to navigate, cluttered with ads, and visually unappealing. I wanted something simple, clean, and user-friendly. Using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I created a minimalist, ad-free interface that makes organizing and sharing personal rankings easy and enjoyable.

Project Lyf180

Lyf180 is a comprehensive health and wellness tracking application designed to help users monitor their fitness, nutrition, and overall lifestyle. The app provides an intuitive dashboard for tracking daily activities, a journal feature for reflection and progress notes, and detailed statistics to visualize trends over time. Built with a focus on user experience and accessibility, Lyf180 combines aesthetic design with powerful functionality to make wellness tracking enjoyable and sustainable.
